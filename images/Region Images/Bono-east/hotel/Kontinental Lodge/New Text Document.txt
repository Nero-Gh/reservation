Kontinental Lodge


Blue Cross Opposite SDA Church, Techiman Ghana

Kontinental lodge is situated in Techiman the Bono East capital in Ghana. The hotel can boast of very spacious car park with 24-hour security services with CCTV . All rooms are spacious and enclosed with bathrooms. Flatscreen TVs with satellite/cable services are provided in all rooms. The hotel provides a bar where alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks can be order.
